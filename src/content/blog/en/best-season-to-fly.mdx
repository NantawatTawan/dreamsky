Chiang Mai has three distinct seasons, and each gives you a completely different flying experience.

## Cool season (November – February)

The best window of the year. Crisp air, clear skies, and fantastic visibility. On some mornings you'll drift above a sea of mist pooling in the valleys — a rare sight.

**Temperature:** 15–25°C
**Recommended:** Fly at 06:30 to catch golden light and morning mist.

## Hot season (March – May)

It's warm, but early morning and late afternoon flights are still great. Watch for haze in March and April — we reschedule if air quality is poor.

**Temperature:** 25–38°C
**Recommended:** Fly at 16:00 for a gold-orange sunset.

## Rainy season (June – October)

Rice fields turn vivid green and the air feels fresh after rain, but the weather needs close watching. Some days flying isn't possible.

**Temperature:** 22–32°C
**Recommended:** Book flexibly — we'll reschedule if it rains.

## The bottom line

For the best experience, aim for **November – February**. But every season has its own character — pick what works for your trip and come fly with us.
