## What is a paramotor?

A paramotor pairs a paraglider wing with a small motor worn on the pilot's back, letting you take off from flat ground — no hill required. It's the most intimate way to fly: no cockpit, no glass, just you and the sky.

## Why Chiang Mai?

Chiang Mai offers a mix of terrain that's hard to match — mountain ranges, rice fields, and morning mist — which is why pilots from around the world come here. Early morning and late afternoon give the smoothest air, making them ideal flight windows.

## What should you bring?

- Long-sleeve shirt, long pants, and ankle-high shoes
- Sunglasses and sunscreen
- An open mind, ready for something new

Don't worry about the flying equipment — our team provides everything: helmet, harness, and certified motor.

## Who can fly?

Anyone 12 years or older, weighing under 110 kg, and without serious medical conditions can fly tandem with a professional pilot — no experience required.

Paramotoring in Chiang Mai is something everyone should try at least once. Whenever you're ready, we're ready to fly with you.
